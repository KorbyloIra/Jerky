Expecially tasty Meat Jerky

Jerky is nutrient-dense meat snack that has been cut into strips and dried (dehydrated) to prevent spoilage.
Normally, this drying includes the addition of salt to prevent bacteria growth before the meat has finished the dehydrating process.
The word "jerky" derives from the Quechua word ch'arki which means "dried, salted meat". All that is needed to produce basic "jerky" is a low-temperature drying method, and salt to inhibit bacterial growth. By removing the moisture from the meat, jerky can survive long periods of time without spoiling. Because of this, jerky is an excellent on-the-go snack.
